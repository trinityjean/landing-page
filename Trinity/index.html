Work

I am still learning different techniques but I really enjoy doing fine-line, anything with color & anything that has to do with nature. Feel free to check out my work on my Instagram!

PRICING- All my work is $60-$80 AT THE MOMENT. My prices will continuously rise. Feel free to message me if you have any questions

Contact Me

In your booking email please include; DESIGN- what would you like tattooed? Please specify as clearly as possible. Please include at least one reference photo so I can design something that you’ll absolutely love. SIZE- please provide the approximate size you are wanting in inches. That will help me determine the pricing and the time allotted. PLACEMENT- please tell me where you would like your tattoo. Also please let me know if you have any other tattoos that I will need to work around. AVAILABILITY- I am in the shop sundays, Monday’s, Tuesdays, Wednesdays, & the occasional Thursday, if none of those days work. I will work with you to find a time that works for both of us.
